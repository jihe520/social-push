## 发布微信公众号文章 workflow (OpenClaw)

1. 打开公众号后台：`browser(action=navigate, targetUrl="https://mp.weixin.qq.com/")`
2. 等待加载：`browser(action=act, request={kind:wait, timeMs:3000})`
3. 点击"文章"新建：`browser(action=act, request={kind:evaluate, fn:"document.querySelector('.new-creation__menu-title').click()"})`
4. 等待编辑器加载：`browser(action=act, request={kind:wait, timeMs:3000})`
5. 查看交互元素：`browser(action=snapshot, refs="aria")`
6. 填写标题：`browser(action=act, request={kind:fill, ref:"标题输入框ref", text:"{标题}"})`
7. 选择作者：`browser(action=act, request={kind:click, ref:"作者框ref"})` → 从下拉选择
8. 点击正文编辑区：`browser(action=act, request={kind:click, selector:"#ueditor_0"})`
9. 输入正文：`browser(action=act, request={kind:type, selector:"#ueditor_0", text:"{正文内容}"})`
10. 上传封面（hover 触发菜单）：`browser(action=act, request={kind:hover, ref:"封面区域ref"})`
11. 点击"从图片库选择"：`browser(action=act, request={kind:evaluate, fn:"document.querySelector('#js_cover_null .js_imagedialog').click()"})`
12. 上传封面图片：`browser(action=upload, selector:"input[type=file][accept*='image/bmp']", paths:["{图片路径}"])`
13. 等待上传：`browser(action=act, request={kind:wait, timeMs:2000})`
14. 完成裁剪确认：按 snapshot 找到"下一步"和"确认"按钮依次点击
15. 保存草稿：`browser(action=act, request={kind:click, ref:"保存为草稿ref"})`
16. 提示用户在公众号后台确认后手动发布

## 注意事项

- 正文编辑器是 UEditor（非 ProseMirror），需用 `type` 输入，不能用 `fill`
- 封面上传需先 hover 到封面区域触发菜单
- 图片库弹窗有两个 `input[type=file]`，封面上传用 `accept*='image/bmp'` 的那个
- 仅保存草稿，由用户手动确认发布
