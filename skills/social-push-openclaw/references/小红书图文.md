## 上传图文 workflow (OpenClaw)

1. 打开发布图文页面：`browser(action=navigate, targetUrl="https://creator.xiaohongshu.com/publish/publish?source=official&from=tab_switch&target=image")`
2. 查看交互元素：`browser(action=snapshot, refs="aria")`
3. 上传图片：`browser(action=upload, ref:"文件上传ref", paths:["{图片路径}"])`
4. 填写标题：`browser(action=act, request={kind:fill, ref:"标题输入框ref", text:"{标题}"})`
5. 填写正文：`browser(action=act, request={kind:fill, selector:".ProseMirror", text:"{正文内容}"})`
6. 添加话题：`browser(action=act, request={kind:type, selector:".ProseMirror", text:" #{话题}"})` → `browser(action=act, request={kind:press, key:"Enter"})`
7. 暂存草稿：`browser(action=act, request={kind:click, ref:"暂存离开ref"})`

## 注意事项

- 标题不要超过 20 字符
- 每步操作后用 `browser(action=snapshot)` 确认元素 ref
